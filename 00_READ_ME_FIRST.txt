HOW IT WORKS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Educator enters lesson topics
2. Clicks "Generate Review Sheet"
3. Claude AI creates complete review:
   - Quick lesson explanation
   - Key concepts summary
   - Real-world examples
   - 4 practice activities (20 questions)
   - Complete answer key
4. User prints or downloads

All in 20-30 seconds! ⚡
